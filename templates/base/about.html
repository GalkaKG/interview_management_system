About Interview Management System

Welcome to the Interview Management System (IMS), a powerful tool designed to streamline and automate the interview process within your organization.

Our Mission

At IMS, our mission is to simplify and enhance the interview management experience. We aim to provide a comprehensive solution that enables your team to efficiently schedule, track, and manage interviews with ease.

Key Features

  • Scheduled Interviews: Easily schedule interviews with candidates and interviewers, keeping everyone informed about the upcoming interviews.
  • Automated Notifications: Receive automated reminders and notifications for scheduled interviews, ensuring that no interview is missed.
  • Status Updates: Track the status of each interview, from "Scheduled" to "Completed," providing transparency and clarity throughout the process.
  • Feedback Management: Collect and manage feedback from interviewers, helping your team make informed decisions.

We appreciate your trust in our system and look forward to helping you streamline your interview management process.
